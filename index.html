SHPE Vice President

(Fall 2014 - Present)

Our Website

With SHPE-MAES, I have been able to impact lives. In the last five years our chapter has grown by almost ten times its original size. With this tremendous growth we have been able to make a difference in the lives of countless students and young adults in the surrounding communities through our community outreach programs such as Noche de Ciencias, Science Extravaganza, and SHPE Jr. Because of these experiences, many of our members have gone on to become great leaders, have traveled the world, been accepted into elite graduate programs, and obtained prestigious internships all across the United States.

Critical Thinkers

(Fall 2014 - Present)

Critical Tinkers Website

Critical Tinkers is a team of undergraduate students in electrical and computer engineering who use and develop our critical thinking skills to create exciting and challenging projects. The goals of our group are to learn to think critically, develop hands on demos, projects, and activities. We take part in high school visitations and perform demonstrations ourselves and help faculty members in the department with their own presentations and demos. We want to be a source of enthusiasm and excitement in the department, and become life-long learners. We believe learning should be interesting, challenging, creative, and engaging.
